About Playboard

Play, Share, and Save

Playboard will be a tool that can be used as a way to acknowledge accomplishments, to play games with friends, to share memories with family and many other ways. It is important for platforms to help people stay in touch with friends and family and also offer the ability to discover new friends and communities. Playboard's goal is to provide an outlet for users to express their creativity.
